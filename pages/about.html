Our Story

Founded in 2024, Lilian Poultry began as a small family farm with just 100 chickens. What started as a way to provide healthy food for our family grew into a passion for sharing high-quality poultry products with our community.

Today, we maintain our commitment to small-scale, ethical farming while serving hundreds of families in our region with fresh eggs and chickens.

Our Farming Practices

Free Range

Chickens roam freely outdoors, foraging naturally and living a stress-free life.

Medication

Chicks are vaccinated with care by certified veterinarians using trusted products.

Natural Feed

We feed our poultry with non-GMO grains and natural forages from their surroundings.

Humane Treatment

Low stocking density and natural enrichment ensure healthy and happy poultry.
